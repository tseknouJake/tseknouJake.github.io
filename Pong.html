Try to beat Pong

CONTROLS

"W" to move up

"S" to move down

OR

"↑" to move up

"↓" to move down

In order to win the AI must NOT score a single goal. Every time you score the AI becomes increasing better and the ball gets faster and faster until you beat the game. Good luck!
